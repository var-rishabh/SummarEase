Amazing Features for you

Extractive

Extractive summarization involves selecting and merging existing sentences or phrases from the source text to create a summary, rather than generating new content.

Abstractive

Abstractive summarization condenses text by interpreting and rephrasing content, aiming for concise summaries similar to human-written ones.

Link Support

"Link support" enables summarization tools to create brief summaries directly from web content accessed via URLs. Useful in news articles.

Fast Response

The swift and rapid generation of summaries by a system or tool, ensuring quick processing and delivery of summarized content in a short period.
